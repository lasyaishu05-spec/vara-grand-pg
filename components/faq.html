Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers for room seekers and parents before calling or visiting.

Is Vara Grand only for women?

Yes, Vara Grand Ultra Premium Women's PG Hostel is a women-only PG in SR Nagar, Hyderabad.

What room sharing options are available?

2 sharing, 3 sharing, and 4 sharing rooms are offered, with AC and non-AC options depending on availability.

Are meals included?

Yes, residents get 3 meals as part of the daily PG routine.

What safety features are provided?

The PG includes CCTV monitoring and 24x7 warden support for a safer women-only living environment.
